Major Prize

This is a game of skill.

  • One (1) major winner will be determined by the total points accrued over the season, as displayed on the Pole Predictor Leaderboard under the participant's email address/Unique username.

The points are calculated as follows:

REASON FOR POINTS

1000 POINTS - For placing a vote per race round

1000 POINTS - For correctly guessing the lap time

500 POINTS - For guessing the driver who ranked 1 in the relevant race

200 POINTS - For guessing the driver who ranked 2 in the relevant race

100 POINTS - For guessing the driver who ranked 3 in the relevant race

0-1000 POINTS - You can score more points based on how close the lap prediction was to the driver’s fastest lap time.

  •  More than 2 seconds (2000 milliseconds) between your lap prediction and the Fastest lap time will score 0 points.
  • The score is based on the distance between your lap prediction and the pole driver’s fastest lap time — then multiplied by the score multiplier for lap time difference.
  • For instance: with  250 milliseconds off lap time, you would score 875 points:
    2000 - 250 = 1750
    1750 x 0.5 = 875
  • If wet tyres are used by a driver during the qualifying session, all lap timing predictions are deemed invalid, and no points will be awarded for that race.
  • In the case of a red flag, or if a qualifying session is ended prematurely with more than 5 minutes remaining, all lap timing predictions are deemed invalid, and no points will be awarded for that race.
