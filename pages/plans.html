Data Usage

Keeping track of data

Keeping track of your data usage has never been easier. You can check your data usage in the My Boost Mobile app, available on iPhone and Android:

  • Open the My Boost Mobile app.
  • Your current total data balance and expiry dates for your service/s are displayed on the screen.
  • Tap on the service number you want to review, and a detailed breakdown of all your inclusions will be displayed.
  • Tap the “Data Breakdown” link below the data bar and your bonus/rollover data balance will be displayed.

Excess data charges

Prepaid plans offer the advantage of avoiding excess data charges. By paying for your data upfront, you won't face any surprise costs even if you run out of data. If you require additional data, you can easily purchase a new recharge, ensuring you have complete control over your data usage at all times.

How many GB data is enough for a month?

We get it—data usage is as unique as your favourite pizza toppings. You want just the right amount of monthly data, without paying for leftovers you won't use. Get to know your data needs by taking a peek at your current data consumption in your phone's settings.

  • Light usage: minimal web browsing, checking emails and messages - get by with up to 3GB of data.
  • Medium usage: frequent use of social media, streaming music and watching videos - requires approximately 5-10GB per month.
  • Heavy usage: extensive video streaming, gaming and downloading large files - requires about +10GB of data per month.
